Michigan State University

I pursued my degree in Media and Information at Michigan State University because I felt the most at home in East Lansing's beautiful campus and the ComArtSci college seemed like a great fit for me. I wanted to go into media and information specifically because there are many opportunities that a business could benefit from MI majors. I want to help improve existing products to become ever better and stand apart from the rest of the competition. I also can see myself gathering data and researching consumers and how they feel towards products or ads. Another career I can see myself doing is media management or a media ambasador because I want to help a business flourish with creative ads or media campaigns.

While at MSU, I have met my greatest friends, experienced sporting wins and losses, and have grown a lot as an individual. I went in blind not knowing anyone freshman year and my luck helped me create amazing connections and lifelong friendships. I am a fan of the bars East Lansing has to offer, and I truly experienced college to the fullest. Over time, I honed my skills in web development and media richness to take my career skills above and beyond.
